Covid-19 Efficacy Dashboard

Dashboard to explore the effect of different vaccine efficacies on Covid-19 breakthrough and protection rates in various countries.

Overview

Breakthrough rates, or the proportion of the population who are vaccinated against infection of Covid-19 but still are susceptible to getting infected, and the percentage of vaccines not offering protection are metrics that are vital to understanding how protected a population is against Covid. However, this information is highly affected by the vaccine efficacy of the different available vaccines.

This dashboard explores breakthrough rates and percentage of vaccines not offering protection in different countries by vaccine variant and Covid-19 variant, over time.

Technical Details: Python (dash, plotly, pandas), CSS
