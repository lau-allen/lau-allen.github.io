Tableau Vaccinations Dashboard

Tableau Dashboard to display Covid-19 total vaccinations in world regions, while exploring color palettes addressing various types of color blindness.

Overview

Effective visualizations are essential for conveying a message clearly to an audience. One aspect of create effective visualizations include choosing the correct color palette to display information clearly, especially for visually impaired people.
Tableau, an industry standard platform for business intelligence and analytics, is used to create color-blind friendly palettes, while exploring Covid-19 vaccination rates for various world regions.
